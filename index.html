High Quality Clothing

Casual clothing in the heart of Wolfeboro.

Wolfeboro Casuals has been a part of downtown Wolfeboro since the last century. Located on Main Street and by the town docks, you can come by boat!

Visitors and residents stop in year round.

Wolfeboro Casuals is open year round with clothing and gifts for every season.

Latest styles for Women.

Discover lakeside chic with our women's summer dresses, purses, and sun hats. Embrace Wolfeboro, New Hampshire's casual elegance in every piece. Elevate your style with fashion designed for effortless lakeside living.

For Kids.

Curate your youngin's wardrobe with our cozy sweatshirts, paired perfectly with a delightful selection of toys and enchanting children's books. Explore our thoughtfully curated New Hampshire collection.

For Men.

Our men's collection features versatile khaki shorts, comfortable button-down sport shirts, and baseball caps. Designed for laid-back days that effortlessly complement the relaxed charm of lakeside living.

Casual Comfort by the Lake.

Step into the calm of lakeside life. Our collections, like ripples on Lake Winnipesaukee, offer comfort and style. From airy shorts to dapper shirts, find a blend of ease and refinement. But heed caution, for beneath the tranquil surface lies a charm that captivates even the wary traveler.
